Meet Aurora

a lifelong learner, creator,
and future app developer

Aurora is especially proud of beginning her college journey at just 14 years old. She earned her associate’s degree in 2009 with honors, a major accomplishment that laid the foundation for her future educational and career goals.

Aurora is now pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Computer Information Systems at Bellevue University. With about two years to go, she is dedicated to completing her degree while balancing her roles as a student, worker, and parent.

In the next five years, Aurora hopes to work as a mobile app developer at Sam’s Club, improving the apps that associates use every day. Her long-term dream is to work from home, giving her the flexibility to spend more quality time with her family while advancing her career in technology.
