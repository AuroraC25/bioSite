Aurora Crippen's life story is one of resilience, determination, and creativity. Born in Alabama, she spent her early childhood with her mother, stepfather, and younger siblings before a major change at the age of eight, when she moved to Mississippi to live with her father and stepmother. Soon after, her older siblings joined the household, making Aurora the oldest of eleven children on her mother's side, the youngest of three on her father's side, and the only child her parents had together. Growing up in such a large and blended family brought both chaos and challenges, but those experiences shaped Aurora into a thoughtful, loyal, and hardworking person who values stability and love above all else.

From an early age, Aurora discovered a love for learning. She began college at just 14 years old and earned her associate's degree with honors in 2009. Now, she is continuing her education by pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Information Systems, with the goal of becoming a mobile app developer. In the next five years, Aurora hopes to work with Sam's Club, improving the apps that associates use daily, and ultimately finding a career that allows her to work from home and spend more quality time with her family. Outside of academics and career ambitions, Aurora finds joy in hobbies that blend creativity and technology. She enjoys reading, knitting, and experimenting with her 3D printer, which her husband gifted her for her 36th birthday. These interests not only give her a creative outlet but also provide opportunities to share handmade and custom creations with her loved ones.
